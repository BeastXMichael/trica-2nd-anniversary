# Anniversary Website Context: Michael and Trisha

## Purpose
Context brief for building a romantic anniversary website. The site is a gift from Michael to Trisha, marking their 2nd anniversary on **16 May 2026**. The site is being created to be delivered remotely, while Trisha is away.

## Where We Are Right Now
* Trisha is currently in **Thailand**.
* Trisha has just started working.
* Michael and Trisha will reunite in approximately **2 months**.
* This anniversary is being celebrated long distance. The site is essentially a long-distance gift, and the writing should acknowledge that distance without dwelling on it.

## The People

### Michael (me)
21 years old. Undergraduate at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Indonesian.

### Trisha (girlfriend)
20 years old. Final year Business student at NTU, Singapore. Indonesian. Just entered the workforce.
Favorite color: blue, white, pink, use this color for website

## Our Story

### How we met
October 2023, at NTU's orientation programme called "Greet Day" (short for Get Together Day). Both of us applied to be Group Leaders (GLs). All upcoming GLs were required to prepare a performance together, known as the "GL performance." We met during that period. A mutual friend introduced us. From the moment we met, I thought she was very pretty, and I kept finding ways to spend more time with her.

### How we got together
I confessed my feelings to her first, because I wanted her to know how I felt before anything else happened. Some time after, I officially asked her to be my girlfriend. In Indonesian, this formal asking is called "tembak."

She said yes on **16 May 2024**.

Today, **16 May 2026**, is our 2nd anniversary.

## Memory Moments

### 1. Annoying her until she breaks (our love language)
The best moments I have with her are when I annoy her until she's completely fed up. I'll come back from outside, suddenly start screaming for no reason, tickle her, throw the bolster (guling) and pillows at her, drop the bolster on the floor on purpose, spill drinks on the floor. She gets more and more annoyed and starts saying "Michael, stop. Michael. Michael, stop, Michael." And I just keep going. Eventually she actually cries, and then I rush in with "sorry, sorry, sorry, sorry, sorry." This is somehow our thing.

### 2. The way she tells stories
She has such a fun way of telling stories. I love listening to her, especially when she's talking about her family (her papa, her mama), or stories about our mutual friends. The way she narrates makes everything feel alive.

### 3. The way she celebrates my birthday
Her birthday celebrations for me are always something else. One year she cooked me a burger from scratch. The effort she puts into making me feel special is unforgettable.

## What I Love About Her

### She's "chillan" (laid-back and real)
She doesn't have a second face. She's the same person in front of everyone. Honest, genuine, no act. I can trust her completely because there's nothing performed about her.

### She has a mommy-like personality
She is incredibly caring and aware of everyone around her. She always knows what people need before they say it. She takes care of her friends. She takes care of me.

## Faith
Faith in God is central to this relationship. Both of us are deeply religious. Whenever we face problems, we pray, and we let God do his work. We trust him fully. Time and time again, we've watched things fall into the right place at the right moment, and we know it's not coincidence. The miracles in our relationship are God's doing.

Our faith is expressed through prayer and worship music. We do not read the Bible together regularly, so **the builder should NOT include Bible verses on the site** unless Michael provides one specifically. The faith thread should come through in gratitude language, prayer references, and worship music.

## Tone and Language Direction
* **Language mix:** Roughly 50/50 English and Indonesian, with occasional Japanese slang and Chinese (for the dolls' names) where it fits. Should sound like the way we actually talk.
* **Emotional register:** Move between playful and romantic. Some sections should make her laugh (annoying-her stories, the dolls, our slang). Some sections should make her cry (faith, gratitude, how I see her, the closing letter).
* **Overall feel:** Sincere, warm, deeply personal. Not generic romantic. The site should feel like only the two of us would understand certain parts.

## Asset Folder
A folder will be supplied containing:
* Photos from 2024 to 2026 (multiple, for galleries and section illustrations)
* A single hero photo for the opening hook
* A closing photo of the two of us together (for the post-letter section)
* An **audio file**, the song to play on the site (background music, should fade in softly and be user-controllable via a play/pause button to handle browser autoplay restrictions)

## Site Specifications

### Opening hook (top of the site)
Three elements at the top, in this order:
1. **Hero photo:** A single photo Michael will supply, displayed prominently.
2. **Headline text:** "Happy 2nd Anniversary, Sayang" (final wording can be styled).
3. **Days counter:** A counter showing **730 days since 16 May 2024**. Ideally calculated live from the date so it stays accurate if she views it later.

### Closing letter (bottom of the site)
The letter below appears as the final long-form section. This is THE emotional payoff. It should feel like a real handwritten letter, presented in a warm typographic style, paced so it reveals slowly as she scrolls or in a dedicated final section. Preserve all Indonesian phrases exactly as written.

### Final element (after the letter, very last thing on the page)
After the letter signature, the very last thing she sees is **a single photo of the two of us together**, supplied in the asset folder. No additional text needed below the photo, the photo is the punctuation mark on the whole site.

### Audio
A background song will be supplied in the asset folder. It should play softly with a clearly visible play/pause control near the top of the page. Default to muted on load to comply with browser autoplay restrictions, then she taps to start.

### Aesthetic direction (builder's call)
Builder may choose the visual aesthetic. Suggested direction: warm, romantic, slightly editorial. Cream, soft gold, dusty pink, deep wine, or warm neutrals are all on the table. Avoid loud neon, harsh contrast, or anything sterile. Typography should feel personal: a serif for headings, a clean sans or a handwritten font for body and the letter. Photo-heavy layouts are encouraged.

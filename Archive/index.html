A letter for you

Dear Trisha,

Thank you so much for being the best partner I could have ever asked for in these past two years. When we first met during the GL performance two and a half years ago, I could never have expected you to become such an important figure in my life. You have been one of my biggest supporters, one of my best friends, and one of the most caring people I've ever known. Terima kasih sayang, untuk semua yang sudah kamu lakukan untukku.

To be honest, without you, my life would not have been this colourful. I wouldn't feel this secure. I wouldn't be this happy. You have been my anchor. When I feel stressed, when I feel anxious, when I feel uncertain, when it feels like everything is doubting me, I know there is you to come back to. I know you will always be by my side, supporting me, giving me the push I need. Thank you for just being present in my life.

Two years, jujur, enggak kerasa sama sekali. It has flown by so fast. And now that you're not here, I really started to feel it. I've lost the presence of someone who was always there when I got back from outside. It feels so strange. I think I need to recalibrate to being alone again. This has only made me more appreciative of you, more grateful for everything you've done in the past two years.

I really enjoyed every single moment we spent together. It doesn't matter whether it was something small, like scrolling Instagram in our room, going to church and praising God together for everything He has given us and how He has guided us through hard times, or just studying side by side. Those small little things mattered the most.

And of course, the bigger memories too. We went to all the museums in Singapore. We did the river cruise. We went to Gardens by the Bay twice (don't ask me why, but twice). We watched Phantom of the Opera. We explored Changi Jewel. We went to River Wonders and watched Zootopia 2 together, such a good movie. We went to Batam to eat real Indonesian food. We even did island hopping to Lazarus Island and Sisters' Island by ferry, which was a wild one. We went to Universal Studios. Every single date was unforgettable. I really hope we can keep going on dates like these for many years to come. Semoga kamu enggak bosan sama aku ya, sayang.

I know we both have our own problems. But don't worry, aku akan push kamu terus ya, Beb. Aku enggak akan biarkan kamu males-males, tidur terus, no way. Sekarang kamu sudah kerja, kamu enggak boleh kayak gitu lagi, oke? Kamu harus fokus, kamu harus kerja lebih keras untuk masa depan kita, untuk anak-anak kita nanti. Semoga cepat dapat kerjaannya, good luck sayang. Jangan lupa minta tolong Tuhan terus dalam mencari pekerjaan. Tapi enjoy dulu di Thailand ya.

All in all, aku sangat bersyukur dengan kehadiranmu in the past two years. I'm so glad you've been by my side, and I cherish every memory we've made. At the same time, I'm really looking forward to what's next. Next year, the next two years, three years, four years. I really hope, first, that we can keep supporting each other no matter the circumstances. Full trust, full support, no half-ass anything. I want both of us to be able to truly rely on each other. And second, I hope we keep relying our relationship on God. He is the greatest, the kindest, and the one who created us all. He will be the one who guides us through this relationship.

Jadi, thank you so much, sayang. I love you. I love you so much. See you in about two months.

Yours,

Michael
